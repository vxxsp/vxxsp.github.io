Guide: How to clean install Windows onto a different drive

If you have any questions, please send a message in #tech-support and ping me or DM me (v.x#0030).

REQUIREMENTS

  • 8GB or higher capacity USB storage device
  • Windows 7 or higher computer (for making bootable disk)
  • Brain

STEPS

PART 1

    1. Download MediaCreationTool.exe from Microsoft here.
    2. Plug in your 8GB or larger USB storage device.
    3. Open Windows Explorer.
    4. Right click your USB drive and press Format.
    5. Format it to NTFS (Quick)
    6. Open the MediaCreationTool.exe file. It should look like this:
    7. After the "Getting a few things ready screen ends", it should look like this. (NOTE: MAKE SURE TO READ THE EULA)
    8. After reading the EULA, press "Create Installation Media" and continue.
    9. Then, select your region, language, and architecture.
    10. After that, select "USB flash drive".
    11. Next, you need to select your 8GB USB storage device and press continue. (to select on it, just press the name)
    12. It is now downloading Windows 10, wait until it is finished.
    13. When it's finished, it will show this:
    14. Continue to Part 2.

PART 2

  1. Plug your USB storage device and enter your device's boot menu.
  2. Boot from your USB storage device in the boot menu.
  3. Windows Setup will now boot.
  4. Here, confirm everything is correct and press Next.
  5. Press Custom.
  6. Select your other drive.
  7. Windows 10 setup will now install Windows on your other drive.
